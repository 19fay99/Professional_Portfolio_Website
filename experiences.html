Custom Mock Database

My team had created a mock database for the St. Johnsbury Country Club in order to keep track of the club members basic information, along with the dinners they would order for specific events held at the country club.

Printed Circuit Boards

In high school I learned how to create PCB's by making a schematic. I would then design and simulate the circuit in Multisim, use a milling machine to cut out the copper boards based on the Multisim design, and lastly solder components to the boards I had created.
